# Fullproof for Mornings - Roadmap

## Overview
**Fullproof for Mornings** is an app designed to help people transform their mornings through curated morning routines. Routines include breathwork, meditation, yoga, qi gong, fitness, journaling, and prayer (optional).
 Users can select from short or long routines, guided by motivational and advocation messaging. The app empowers users to create sustainable, 
beneficial habits that lead to vitality and mindfulness—eventually becoming self-sufficient in their morning rituals.

---

## Milestones

### 1. **Phase 1: Planning & Research**
   - [ ] Finalize feature set based on user personas and target audience.
   - [ ] Research and curate routines (short, long, flexible).
   - [ ] Collect YouTube videos (breathwork, yoga, qi gong, etc.) for integration.
   - [ ] Plan brand messaging and tone (encouraging, spiritual, but non-intrusive).

### 2. **Phase 2: Core Development**
   - [ ] Build basic app structure (navigation, UI wireframes).
   - [ ] Integrate Morning Routine Guides (long-form, short-form routines).
     - [ ] Develop step-by-step guides.
     - [ ] Add equipment lists per routine.
   - [ ] Build Morning Routine Generator (suggest activities with or without time constraints).
   - [ ] Implement the "Advocate" text system (motivational and spiritual guidance).

### 3. **Phase 3: Video Integration & Randomization**
   - [ ] Create a video library for guided routines.
   - [ ] Integrate YouTube videos into the app with options to play them in a specific or random order.
   - [ ] Add flexibility for user preference (breathwork-only, meditation-only routines).

### 4. **Phase 4: User Experience Enhancements**
   - [ ] Refine the user interface (UI) to create a smooth and encouraging flow.
   - [ ] Test the Morning Routine Generator to ensure users receive varied suggestions.
   - [ ] Implement evening routine support, encouraging users to take part before sleep.

### 5. **Phase 5: Beta Testing & Feedback**
   - [ ] Release the app to a closed beta group for feedback.
   - [ ] Gather insights from users on routine preferences, app usability, and messaging.
   - [ ] Adjust routine guides, videos, and generator based on feedback.

### 6. **Phase 6: Launch**
   - [ ] Finalize the app for launch with a polished UI and complete feature set.
   - [ ] Prepare for a soft launch and marketing campaign.
   - [ ] Launch **Fullproof for Mornings** across app stores.

### 7. **Stretch Goals (Post-Launch)**
   - [ ] Allow users to submit their own YouTube videos or routines for future guides.
   - [ ] Introduce progress tracking (days completed, streaks, etc.).
   - [ ] Partner with health and fitness influencers for more content and exposure.

## Phases Breakdown

### **Phase 1: Planning & Research**
   - Goals: 
     - Understand the target audience and curate meaningful routines.

   - Deliverables:
     - Routine guides for short, long, flexible plans.

### **Phase 2: Core Development**
   - Goals: 
     - Build and refine the structure of the app, ensuring users can easily navigate and choose routines.

     - Functional Morning Routine Generator.

### **Phase 3: Video Integration & Randomization**
   - Goals:
     - Integrate YouTube videos with step-by-step routines.
     - Add flexibility in playing the videos (specific or random order).

   - Goals:
     - Improve the app’s flow and usability.

## Metrics for Success
- **Engagement:** Track how often users complete morning routines.
- **User Retention:** Measure how many users continue to use the app after the first week.
- **Routine Generator Usage:** Analyze how often users engage with the Morning Routine Generator.
- **Feedback:** Qualitative feedback on how the app influences their mornings and general wellbeing.
- **Spiritual Engagement:** Track how many users engage with the optional spiritual guidance aspects of the routines.

## Potential Risks & Challenges
- **Video Licensing:** Ensuring all YouTube videos used in the app have proper rights or permissions.
- **User Burnout:** Finding the right balance of motivation without overwhelming users with content.
- **Complexity:** Managing the complexity of routine generation while keeping the app user-friendly.
- **Feedback Iteration:** Balancing user feedback with staying true to the app's core message and brand tone.

## Conclusion
**Fullproof for Mornings** is positioned to help people own their mornings, creating habits that bring vitality, mindfulness, and spiritual awareness. By providing curated routines, 
motivational messaging, and a flexible generator, this app will empower users to transform their mornings and eventually live without boundaries. The journey starts now—Carpe Diem for mornings!
